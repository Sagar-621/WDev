Shipping & Returns

Everything customers need to know about dispatch timelines, delivery expectations, cancellations, and returns on DEVASTHRA.

Dispatch Window Usually within 1-3 business days after order confirmation.
Shipping Coverage Pan-India shipping subject to courier serviceability for the delivery pincode.
Returns Available only for eligible items within the active return window after delivery.

Shipping Policy

  • Orders are processed after payment confirmation or successful placement for Cash on Delivery.
  • Delivery timelines depend on inventory, pickup scheduling, and courier serviceability.
  • Once shipped, tracking details are available on your DEVASTHRA order pages.
  • In rare cases, dispatch may be delayed during sale periods, launches, festive drops, or logistics disruptions.

Cancellation Policy

  • Orders can usually be cancelled before they move to packing or shipping.
  • Once an order is packed, shipped, or delivered, cancellation may no longer be available.
  • Refund timelines for prepaid orders depend on the original payment method and bank processing cycle.

Returns & Exchanges

  • Returns are allowed only on eligible products and only within the configured return window after delivery.
  • Products must be unused, unwashed, and returned with original packaging, tags, and accessories wherever applicable.
  • Non-returnable categories, hygiene-sensitive products, or promotional items may not qualify for returns.
  • If approved, refunds are processed back to the original payment method or according to the order’s payment mode.

Need Help?

If you need help with shipping, delivery, returns, or exchanges, use the Order Tracking page, your My Orders dashboard, or contact our support team through the website contact form.
