Customer Help

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about ordering, payment, sizing, shipping, and returns on DEVASTHRA.

How do I choose the right size?

Use the Size Guide on the product page before selecting your size. Compare your chest, waist, hip, and shoulder measurements with the chart that matches the garment type.

Can I place an order without logging in?

You need to sign in before checkout so we can link your order, shipment, and support history securely to your account.

Which payment methods are available?

Depending on the product and delivery location, DEVASTHRA supports prepaid checkout and Cash on Delivery where available.

How do I track my order?

You can track your shipment from Order Tracking or directly from My Orders after login.

How long does shipping take?

Standard domestic orders are usually dispatched in 1-3 business days. Delivery timelines depend on your pincode and courier serviceability.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can usually be cancelled while they are still in `Pending` or `Paid` status. Once packed or shipped, cancellation may no longer be available.

Do you accept returns or exchanges?

Eligible products can be returned within the configured return window after delivery. Return availability can vary by product type and category.

How do I contact support?

You can use the contact form on the homepage, the support chat on the website, or write to support@devasthra.com.
