Size Guide

Use these standard measurement charts to choose the right fit before you order. For the best result, compare your body measurements with the table that matches your garment type.

How To Measure

  • Chest / Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest or bust while keeping the tape level.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, just above the navel.
  • Hip: Measure around the widest part of your hips with feet together.
  • Shoulder: For shirts and structured tops, measure shoulder point to shoulder point across your back.
  • Length: For longline or ethnic styles, compare with the garment length shown on the product page.
